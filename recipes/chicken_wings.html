Chicken Wings !!!



Description :

Chicken wings are a popular appetizer or snack, often served fried or baked.

They can be coated in a variety of sauces, such as buffalo, barbecue, or honey garlic, and are typically served with celery sticks and dipping sauces like blue cheese or ranch.

Ingredients

  • Chicken wings
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Paprika
  • Flour (for coating, if frying)
  • Oil (for frying, if frying)
  • Buffalo sauce
  • Barbecue sauce
  • Honey garlic sauce
  • Celery sticks
  • Blue cheese or ranch dressing (for dipping)

Steps to Prepare Chicken Wings

  1. Preheat your oven to 400째F (200째C) or heat oil in a deep fryer to 375째F (190째C) if frying.
  2. Season the chicken wings with salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and paprika.
  3. If frying, coat the wings lightly in flour. Fry the wings in batches for about 8-10 minutes, or until golden brown and crispy. Drain on paper towels.
  4. If baking, place the seasoned wings on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake for 40-45 minutes, turning halfway through, until crispy and cooked through.
  5. In a large bowl, toss the cooked wings with your choice of sauce (buffalo, barbecue, honey garlic, etc.) until evenly coated.
  6. Serve the wings hot with celery sticks and a side of blue cheese or ranch dressing for dipping.
