French Fries !!!



Description :

French fries are a beloved side dish made from potatoes that are cut into strips and deep-fried until crispy.

They can be seasoned with salt or various spices and are often served with ketchup, mayonnaise, or other dipping sauces.

Ingredients

  • Russet potatoes
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)
  • Optional seasonings (e.g., paprika, garlic powder, onion powder)
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonnaise
  • Other dipping sauces (optional)

Steps to Prepare French Fries

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into thin strips.
  2. Soak the potato strips in cold water for at least 30 minutes to remove excess starch.
  3. Drain the potatoes and pat them dry with a clean towel.

  7. Remove the fries from the oil and drain on paper towels. Immediately season with salt and any optional seasonings.
  8. Serve the French fries hot with ketchup, mayonnaise, or other dipping sauces of your choice.
